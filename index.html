About LUMENS

LUMENS is a framework based on user-friendly parsimonious and publicly available software. It was specifically developed to empower multi-stakeholder negotiations for planning sustainable landscapes that can support livelihoods and development while maintaining and restoring environmental services especially in tropical countries.

Through its suite of interactive web applications, LUMENS empowers users to explore trade-offs, identify synergies, and develop sustainable land use strategies that promote the long-term health and well-being of our planet.
